About Me

My Background

Hello! I'm Dai Jimenez, a UI Designer with a background in Architecture & Interior Design. After graduating from East Carolina University in December of 2018 with my Bachelor of Science in Interior Design, I went off to work in the Architecture & Design industry. But just like any other industry: we took a hit when the pandemic began. Fortunately, I was able to take this time to advance and broaden my skills as a designer by taking the UI Design Program from CareerFoundry.

My professional background has equipped with me solid practical skills combined with a deep understanding of design fundamentals spanning a variety of media. I am eager to dive back into the design sphere and apply my newfound skills, knowledge, and perspective. Thanks for getting to know a little bit more about me!
